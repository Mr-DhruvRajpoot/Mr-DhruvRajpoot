## 🔬 Case Studies

### [Activation Optimisation — Bank Campaign A/B Analysis](https://github.com/Mr-DhruvRajpoot/bank-campaign-activation-analysis)

**Problem** &nbsp; Only 32% of users completed onboarding — KYC was the activation bottleneck.

**Analysis** &nbsp; Funnel breakdown by step, segmented by session time at KYC. Users spending >5 min converted at 3–4× the average. Chi-square testing validated segment-level significance (p < 0.05). March, September, October identified as peak conversion windows.

**Impact** &nbsp; Guided KYC flow + friction reduction simulated **+14pp activation lift**.

`Python` `scipy` `Chi-square testing` `Funnel analysis` `Segmentation`

---

### [Churn Diagnostics & Retention Strategy](https://github.com/Mr-DhruvRajpoot/employee-churn-prediction)

**Problem** &nbsp; High attrition raising replacement cost. Business needed who churns, when, and why — before spending on retention.

**Analysis** &nbsp; **Who:** Sales and junior roles at 2.4× base rate. **When:** 12–18 month tenure. **Why:** Overtime + low satisfaction = near-certain exit. High-satisfaction overtime workers stayed.

**Impact** &nbsp; Retention spend targeted at highest-risk cohort. Random Forest (ROC-AUC **0.78**, 74% precision) ranks individuals for intervention — model comes last, after the diagnosis.

`scikit-learn` `Random Forest` `Cohort analysis` `Feature importance` `ROC-AUC`

---

### [Sales Reporting Automation & Data Pipeline](https://github.com/Mr-DhruvRajpoot/Sales-reporting-automation-)

**Problem** &nbsp; 4.5 hours of weekly manual Excel reporting blocked growth decisions — reports arrived too late to act.

**Impact** &nbsp; Python pipeline replaced the full workflow. IQR anomaly detection, intelligent imputation, auto-generated executive summaries. Zero code changes for new periods or regions. Execution: **4.5 hrs → 2 seconds**.

`Python` `pandas` `NumPy` `matplotlib` `ETL`

### [KPI Hierarchy & Growth Measurement — 155K Transactions](https://github.com/Mr-DhruvRajpoot/retail-kpi-growth-dashboard)

**Problem** &nbsp; Growth looked healthy at the top line — some regions were masking underperformance.

**Impact** &nbsp; KPI framework built from company → region → category → SKU. Lagging regions isolated for reallocation. Revenue per customer varied **up to 3×** across regions.

`Python` `pandas` `KPI Framework` `YoY Analysis`

---

### [Revenue Yield & CAC Efficiency — Flight Booking Segments](https://github.com/Mr-DhruvRajpoot/flight-booking-cac-analysis)

**Problem** &nbsp; Marketing budget spread evenly — no visibility into which segments had the best revenue-per-acquisition.

**Insight** &nbsp; Business class on short-haul routes: 60% higher yield per seat. Early bookers (30–90 days) pay premium fares.

**Decision** &nbsp; Reallocate toward short-haul business and early-booking economy. Deprioritise last-minute economy campaigns.

`Python` `pandas` `Revenue analysis` `CAC modelling`

### [10-Year CHD Risk Prediction — Framingham Study](https://github.com/Mr-DhruvRajpoot/framingham-chd-analysis)

End-to-end predictive model on 4,238 patients (15% positive rate). Gradient Boosting achieved ROC-AUC 0.75 on a highly imbalanced dataset. Demonstrates modelling rigour in compliance-sensitive, high-stakes environments.

`scikit-learn` `Gradient Boosting` `Class imbalance handling` `EDA`
